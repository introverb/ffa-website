Become a Patron.

Back the foundation building a more optimistic future.

Most visions of the future on offer right now are bleak, impersonal, and ultimately inhuman. FFA exists to fund the other kind. We support the artists, scientists, and writers who can picture a future worth wanting and make it real enough to build toward.

A patron backs the foundation itself, not a single project. Your support funds Possibilia, the OURS exhibition, and the programs that follow. You join at the start, when it counts for the most.

What your patronage does
  • Lists you as a Patron in every new issue of Possibilia, on the patrons & sponsors page.
  • Invites you, with a guest, to OURS and future FFA events.
  • Adds you to the invitation list for private dinners and events.
  • Opens a direct line to the foundation and first word on what we build next.
  • Makes real, tax-deductible support of a 501(c)(3).
