OURS

An exhibition & salon evening · August 9, 2026 · New York City

Many of today's visions of the future are bleak, impersonal, and ultimately inhuman. OURS is our answer to that — an exhibition and salon evening bringing together artists, scientists, writers, and builders around a shared conviction: the future isn't fixed, and the people in this room are among those who get to say so.

Art has always had the power to support and elevate scientific discovery and technological progress, not as decoration but as the cultural infrastructure that makes new ideas legible, desirable, and lasting. To sponsor OURS is to be a patron of that future: to put your name beside the work of artists who are doing for tomorrow what art has always done for the present.

Artists we are working with

Confirmed and in-progress works include pieces by RERO, the French conceptual artist whose work has shown at the Centre Pompidou; Cyborg4rtist, presenting at the Venice Biennale 2026; and we are in conversations with SCI-Arc's Robot House about a special fabricated piece for the exhibition. Additional works are being commissioned from artists working across sculpture, digital fabrication, CGI, print, and architecture.
